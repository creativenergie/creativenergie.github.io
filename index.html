Unlocking Sustainable Energy For Life

Empowering Communities

3 billion people still cook on solid smoky solid fuel and more people die from wood smoke related illnesses than HIV and maleria combined. We partner with and empower churches and organisations in the Global South to bring clean sustainable energy to their communities. The church is deeply embedded within communities and by working this way we can have greater lasting impact at the grass roots level.

Training

We provide bespoke training in small scale renewable energy technologies and kingdom business mentoring. We have significant experience of working with biogas in East Africa.

Partnering

We form long term partnerships with churches and organisations who are embedded within communities in the Global South, empowering them to effectively bring clean energy to their people.

Innovation

We develop and prototype new technologies to help accelerate energy access. For example, we catalysed and lead a consoritum to develop the Smart Biogas Network.
